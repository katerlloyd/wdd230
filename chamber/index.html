Our Mission

Our purpose is to provide a place for local businesses and organizations to come together and improve the quality of life for those we collectively serve in the community. The Boise Idaho Chamber of Commerce is driven by the needs of the people and aims to unite organizations in promoting these needs. We also strive to protect the interests of each business and enact policies surrounding those needs and interests for the furthering of common objectives and, most importantly, serving the people. We supply a place for businesses to locate policies and current issues, find news and weather for the area, and advertise to potential consumers and clients.
